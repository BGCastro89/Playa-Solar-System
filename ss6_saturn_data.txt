Times AFTER 1962 are in UTC, the currentcivil or "wall-clock" time-scale. UTC is kept within 0.9 seconds of UT1by introduction of integer leap-seconds for 1972 and later.  Conversion from the internal Barycentric Dynamical Time (TDB) to thenon-uniform civil UT time-scale requested for output has not been determinedfor UTC times after the next July or January 1st. Therefore, the last knownleap-second is used as a constant over future intervals.  Time tags refer to the UT time-scale conversion on Earth regardless ofwhere the observer is located in the solar system.
